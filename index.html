    ZoomIN: Into the monde, to find globlogs

TEAM MEMBERS

KiranSandilya Aryan Kilaru NagaNitishChandra Pothukuchi Kattalyn Abhishek Sharma

Problem Statement

In a world where we're all more connected than ever, there's still something missing. We want to explore the globe from our screens, connect with people from all over, and get a real taste of different cultures. But the usual social media platforms just aren't cutting it. Sites like Facebook or Instagram show snapshots, but they don’t dive deep into the heart of cultures or hidden gems in various countries. They can't give us that immersive global experience we crave. We need a fun and interactive world blog where we can zoom in on a map, discover cool blogs from different places, and feel like we're traveling without leaving our couches. It's time for a platform that lets us truly explore, connect, and share our world.

Project descripiton

Jet-lag? No more! We want to feel updated without the jet lag. Give us a way to virtually roam the world to uncover blogs and stories from all over.Global Squad: We're all about connecting with folks from diverse backgrounds. We want to make you the blogger who shares their unique experiences and perspectives. Make It Our Own: We dig customization. Let us jazz up our experience with cool options like changing colors, backgrounds, and making the site feel unique and represent our culture and traditions! Hottest Hits: We want to know what's trending and popular in specific parts of the world, so bring on the voting and rankings.

User Goals

Users can find a globe on the landing page and can choose any place to explore it virtually.Users can write blogs about the places they have been to, or they live in.The UI of this website is going to be distinct and unique for a better user experience.The dynamics of this website can be felt right from the start when users enter the landing pageUsers can see a variety of feedback for almost every action like animations, sounds, Themes, etc.The main attraction is, users can move the globe around zoom into a city, and click on the blog they like among many others.Can save the blogs for future reference.Users can comment and like blogs of others.Users can have real-time scenarios around the world.Users can join the discussion forums and know the hottest gossip around the world. can be more helpful for niche searches.

User Actions

Information on the destination: Users can easily zoom in places for uncovering information about the different corners in the world.Users can access a list of the bloggers that share their experiences around different locations by the app! Use our hashtag feature! Just type WHATEVER you want after the “#” and connect with the like-minded individuals in every imaginable corner of the globe!Top attractions: You can decide what is popping in your neighborhood by using our voting system and let your favorite thing to be the next trending blog!

Potential Obstacles

Accuracy: Users might be provided with faulty information.Language Barrier: Users might come across blogs in various languages making it difficult for them.Information Overload: Users might get loads of information which will make them experience inconveniences.Toxic blogs: Users can come across unwanted, hateful, and disrespectful blogs that can be mentally harmful user experience.
